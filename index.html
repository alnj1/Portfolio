About Me

Hey! I'm Allen John Jojo, a passionate Computer Science & Engineering student at Vimal Jyothi Engineering College. I love turning ideas into working systems — whether that's through clean code or creative visual work.

My technical journey revolves around Python, where I enjoy solving problems algorithmically and building automation scripts. I'm always exploring new libraries and frameworks to expand what I can build.

Beyond code, I'm an artist at heart. I sketch, illustrate, and experiment with digital art as a way to think visually and creatively. Design and engineering don't have to be separate worlds.

I also have a deep fascination with geography — maps, geopolitics, cultures, and how physical landscapes shape human stories. It keeps me curious about the world beyond the screen.
